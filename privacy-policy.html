Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Your Budget ("the App") handles your information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data you enter in the App (budgets, expenses, savings goals, reminders) is stored locally on your device only.

Data Storage

All your financial data is stored locally on your device using the device's built-in storage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • No data is sent to any server or cloud service
  • No account or registration is required
  • Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all your data

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No third parties have access to your data.

Permissions

The App does not require any special device permissions such as camera, location, contacts, or internet access for its core functionality.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since no data is collected from any user, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
